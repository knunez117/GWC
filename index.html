About Me:

Hi there! I am a stationary aficionada who loves note taking and making every note I take as extra as possible. I love being creative in my everyday life and note taking is the perfect place to let my creativity flow. I discovered the Studygram and Studyblr communities during my freshman year of high school and I was inspired to try new formating for my notes and also developed a love for calligraphy and lettering! Just like I was inspired by others, I hope I can inspire you!
